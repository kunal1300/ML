The President of India is the head of the country.
The current President is Droupadi Murmu.
The President lives in the Rashtrapati Bhavan.
The President is the supreme commander of the Indian Armed Forces.
The President is elected by members of Parliament and State Legislatures.
The term of the President is five years.
The President appoints the Prime Minister and other ministers.
The President gives assent to laws passed by Parliament.
The President can declare emergencies in the country.
